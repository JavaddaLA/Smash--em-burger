Our story

BORN FROM THE GRIDDLE

It started with a cast iron skillet and one simple rule: smash it hard, cook it fast, never look back.

We took the classic smash technique — high heat, maximum crust, perfect caramelization — and made it the whole religion. No shortcuts, no freezer patties, no gimmicks. Just fresh-ground beef, a screaming hot flat-top, and a whole lot of pressure.

Every burger leaves that griddle with crispy, lacy edges and a juicy center that'll have you ordering a second before you're done with your first.
